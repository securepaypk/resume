General Questions

What is DigiSecurePay?

DigiSecurePay is a digital payment platform that offers smart payment solutions and efficient transport services in Pakistan. It integrates advanced AI, eco-friendly transport options, and enhanced security features.

How do I make a payment using DigiSecurePay?

You can make payments through our app using various methods such as mobile wallets, credit/debit cards, or offline payment options like QR codes and SMS-based transactions.

Is DigiSecurePay safe to use?

Yes, DigiSecurePay uses advanced encryption, fraud detection systems, and wallet insurance to ensure your transactions and personal data are secure.

How do I get rewards for eco-friendly transport?

Simply use eco-friendly transport options, and you will earn cashback and rewards through our Green Transport Initiative. Check your rewards balance in the app.
